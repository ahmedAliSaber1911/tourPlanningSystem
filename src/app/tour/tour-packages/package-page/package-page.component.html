Package Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Cairo: Arrival to Cairo Aiport

Welcome to Cairo airport! The first day of your tour begins when you are greeted by our representative who will meet you and assist you through immigration and customs formalities upon your arrival. After you have collected your luggage, he will then take you in our deluxe vehicle to your 5-star hotel in Cairo. Hotel check-in and overnight.

2

Day 2 - Cairo: Giza Pyramids, Sphinx, Egyptian Museum

Breakfast at the hotel is followed by a full-day tour. In the morning you will be driven to see the Pyramids of Giza and the Great Sphinx: the most famous monuments of not only ancient Egypt but of the entire ancient world! In the afternoon you will be taken to the Egyptian Museum: gaze at the wonders of King Tutankhamun, including his golden death mask; lose yourself in the many rooms and galleries, where thousands of artifacts are awaiting your visit. At the end of this exciting day, you will be driven back to your hotel.

3

Day 3 - Flight to Luxor, Nile Cruise: Luxor (Karnak & Luxor Temples)

After breakfast, you will check-out from the hotel and our representative will escort you to the airport for your flight to Luxor. Upon arrival, you will be met and assisted by our representative who will transfer you to your cruise boat: your floating hotel for your next 4 nights. After lunch, which you will have onboard, you will visit the magnificent Karnak and Luxor Temples. Once your visit to these ancient Theban Temples is complete, you will return to the boat for your dinner.

Breakfast

4

Day 4 - Nile Cruise: Luxor (Westbank: Kings Valley, and Hatshepsut Temple

After your first breakfast on board, it is time to visit the West Bank of Luxor: the tombs of the New Kingdom Pharaohs in the Valley of the Kings; the tombs of princes in the Valley of the Queens; the magnificent temple of Queen Hatshepsut at Deir El-Bahri finishing with the immense Colossi of Memnon. After this visit to the Theban "City of the Dead", you will be taken back to your boat for your lunch, and then you can watch as it departs Luxor and sails for Esna. As the Nile flows gently underneath you, dinner will be served.

Breakfast
Lunch

5

Day 5 - Nile Cruise: Edfu Temple

You can enjoy your breakfast watching as the cruise boat crosses the Esna lock and then sails onward to Edfu, home of the best-preserved temple in Egypt: the temple of Edfu or Temple of Horus. Your guide will then return you to your cruise boat for dinner as the boat sails majestically toward Kom Ombo.

Meals
Breakfast
Lunch
Dinner

6

Day 6 - Nile Cruise: Kom Ombo Temple, Aswan (High Dam, Philae Temple)

Your last day of sailing starts with breakfast, and then a visit to the Temple of Kom Ombo dedicated to the gods Horus and Sobek. Afterward, it is back to the boat for lunch and then sails to Aswan. Once the boat arrives in Aswan, you will be driven to see the High Dam, the world's largest rock-filled dam, and the Temple of Philae, which was moved to the new island of Agilika after the waters of Lake Nasser flooded its original location on Philae. Today will be your last dinner on board.

Day 7 - Nile Cruise: Aswan (Botanical Garden), Flight to Cairo

You will have your final breakfast on the boat before you have to check out. You will then be taken to Aswan Botanical Gardens by felucca. After this, you will be transferred to Aswan airport for your flight back to Cairo (the flight duration is about 1 hour only). Once there; our representative will meet you and assist you with your luggage before transferring you to your hotel in Cairo for check-in.

Day 8 - Cairo: Final Departure

Today is your final day which starts with breakfast at the hotel followed by check-out. Our team will be picking you up from your hotel and transfer you to Cairo airport for your final departure back home.
